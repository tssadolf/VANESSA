About Us

" Responding to the movement of planting trees for a green, clean and beautiful environment, returning green to the earth and the environment, the VANESSA brand was born. In order to improve the quality of life, VANESSA will provide mini cacti, cute cactus to create inspiration for home decoration as well as love bonsai from the most familiar and small places.


VANESSA will support every customer to design their own layout or help them design beautiful potted plants, suitable for each person's personality. VANESSA will guide customers how to plant and care for trees so that they can prolong the life of the tree for the longest time. In addition, our store also sells tools, supplies, fertilizers, nutrients for plants, etc. to help customers grow trees more easily.


Planting trees in many places helps protect the shock environment of everyone and every home, so stone lotus flowers are also brought as meaningful gifts for our loved ones, helping them have a healthier living environment. Purify the atmosphere, dispel worries and troubles in life. . In terms of feng shui, the stone lotus is also a symbol of luck, wealth and prosperity.


Thank you for joining us at the table,
Vanessa's Team "
