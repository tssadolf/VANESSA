Echeveria

$20

    The leaves are purple, silver-gray to pinkish-white, depending on how much light the plant absorbs. The leaves are usually up to 8cm long and about 4cm wide. The leaves fold together like the petals of a chrysanthemum.
Symbolizes a faithful love that does not change over time. Purple chrysanthemums also symbolize filial piety, also has auspicious feng shui meaning, bringing fortune and wealth to owners. 

Scientific name:

Corpuscularia Lehmannii

Origin:

Mexico


How to take care of:

Water consumption per day: Only need to water the plants twice or three a week, each time water the plants does not need to be too much.
Fertilizer and nutrients: It's can live in harsh environments. However, you should also add nutrients to the plants so the plants can grow well and be durable, added to the soil with sawdust, coir, or other organic fertilizers, etc. It is possible to supplement nutrients for plants with decaying cow manure or slow-dissolving fertilizers to better growth and development
Lights: It's a sun-loving species so it needs to be exposed to the sun every day. When take the plants bask in the sun, you need to avoid midday hours to avoid burning leaves. As for rainy days, you just need to put the pot in a well-ventilated and lighted place.
